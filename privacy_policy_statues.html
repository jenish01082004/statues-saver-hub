Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 04, 2025

Thank you for using StatuSave (“the App”). This page explains how we handle information when you use the App.

What the App does

StatuSave helps you view and save status media (images/videos) available on your device from messaging apps. It is an independent utility and is not affiliated with, endorsed, or sponsored by WhatsApp Inc. or any other third party.

Data we collect

  • We do not collect personal data from you. The App does not require you to create an account and does not upload your photos, videos, contacts, or other personal content to our servers.
  • On-device access: To function, the App requests access to read and save media files that you select. This access is used only locally on your device to display and store chosen media.
  • Network access: The App uses the Internet connection to load ads and basic app resources.

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob and its partners may collect device identifiers and usage data to provide and improve ad services. For details, see Google’s Privacy Policy and AdMob Policy. You can manage ad personalization in your Google settings.

Permissions

  • Photos/Media – to read and save status images/videos that you choose.
  • Network – to check connectivity and load ads.

Note: Recent Android versions use scoped storage and granular media permissions (e.g., READ_MEDIA_IMAGES/READ_MEDIA_VIDEO). We do not need access to all files on your device.

Data sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data. No user content is transmitted to our servers. Third-party ad providers may process limited data as described above.

Children’s privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Security

The App performs all media handling on-device. Any data transmitted for ads is sent over encrypted connections provided by the ad network.

Intellectual property & fair use

Please respect others’ rights. Only save media for which you have permission. StatuSave is a tool for personal use and must not be used to infringe copyrights or terms of service of third-party platforms.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
